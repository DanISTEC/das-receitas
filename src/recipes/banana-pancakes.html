Banana Pancakes

Fluffy and delicious banana pancakes made with ripe bananas, perfect for a healthy breakfast. These pancakes are naturally sweetened and packed with flavor.

prep time

10 min

cook time

15 min

serving

4 servings

Tags : breakfast vegetarian banana

instructions

step 1

In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, then whisk until well combined.

step 2

Add the flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon to the banana mixture. Stir gently until just combined - do not overmix. The batter should be slightly lumpy.

step 3

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Lightly grease with butter or cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.

step 4

Cook until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set, about 2-3 minutes. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.

step 5

Serve warm with maple syrup, fresh berries, or a sprinkle of powdered sugar. Enjoy your delicious banana pancakes!

ingredients

2 ripe bananas, mashed

2 large eggs

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

2 tablespoons butter or oil for cooking

tools

Large mixing bowl

Fork for mashing bananas

Whisk

Non-stick skillet or griddle

Measuring cups and spoons

Spatula for flipping
