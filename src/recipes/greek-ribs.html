Greek Ribs

Tender beef ribs slow-cooked with Mediterranean herbs and spices. This traditional Greek recipe features fall-off-the-bone tender meat infused with aromatic herbs, garlic, and lemon. Perfect for a hearty family dinner with authentic Mediterranean flavors.

prep time

45 min

cook time

2 hours

serving

4 servings

Tags : beef dinner

instructions

step 1

In a small bowl, combine Greek seasoning, salt, pepper, dried oregano, and dried thyme. Pat the beef ribs dry with paper towels, then generously season all sides with the spice mixture.

step 2

Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot over medium-high heat. Working in batches, sear the ribs on all sides until browned, about 3-4 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate.

step 3

In the same pot, add chopped onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant. Pour in beef broth and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom.

step 4

Return the ribs to the pot, along with lemon juice, bay leaves, and fresh herbs. The liquid should come about halfway up the ribs. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.

step 5

Simmer covered for 2-2.5 hours, turning the ribs occasionally, until the meat is very tender and easily pulls away from the bone. Check the liquid level periodically and add more broth if needed.

step 6

Remove the ribs from the pot and let them rest for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, simmer the cooking liquid uncovered for 10-15 minutes to reduce and thicken the sauce. Serve the ribs with the reduced sauce.

ingredients

3 lbs beef ribs (short ribs or back ribs)

2 tbsp Greek seasoning

2 large onions, chopped

6 cloves garlic, minced

3 cups beef broth

1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

2 tbsp olive oil

2 bay leaves

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp dried thyme

1 tsp kosher salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped (optional)

tools

Large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot

Sharp chef's knife

Cutting board

Kitchen tongs

Wooden spoon

Measuring cups and spoons

Paper towels

Small mixing bowl

Lid for pot
