Carne Asada

Traditional Mexican grilled beef, marinated with citrus and spices for authentic flavor. This tender and flavorful dish is perfect for tacos, burritos, or served as a main course. The marinade infuses the meat with bright citrus notes and warm spices, creating a perfect balance of flavors that's both bold and refreshing.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

serving

4 servings

Tags : beef dinner

instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl, combine lime juice, orange juice, minced garlic, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined to create the marinade.

step 2

Place the flank steak in a large resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Pour the marinade over the beef, ensuring it's completely coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight for maximum flavor.

step 3

Remove the beef from the refrigerator 30 minutes before grilling to bring it to room temperature. Preheat your grill to high heat (450-500°F). Clean and oil the grill grates to prevent sticking.

step 4

Remove the beef from the marinade and pat it completely dry with paper towels. This step is crucial for achieving a good sear. Discard the used marinade.

step 5

Place the beef on the hot grill and cook for 5-7 minutes per side for medium-rare (internal temperature should reach 135°F). For medium, cook 7-9 minutes per side (145°F). Avoid flipping more than once to maintain juiciness.

step 6

Transfer the grilled beef to a cutting board and let it rest for 5-10 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat. Slice against the grain into thin strips for the most tender texture.

ingredients

2 lbs flank steak (or skirt steak)

1/4 cup fresh lime juice

1/4 cup fresh orange juice

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp chili powder

1/2 tsp dried oregano

1/4 cup olive oil

1 tsp kosher salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)

tools

Gas or charcoal grill

Large mixing bowl

Resealable plastic bag or shallow dish

Sharp chef's knife

Cutting board

Paper towels

Meat thermometer

Grill brush

Tongs
