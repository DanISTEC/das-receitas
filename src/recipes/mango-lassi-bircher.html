Mango Lassi Bircher

Nadiya's overnight oats are definitely a wake-up call. If you love mangoes, you must try her tropical vegan bircher muesli recipe. A breakfast worth waiting all night for!

5 min.

5 min.

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Dry fry the flaked almonds in a small frying pan over a medium-high heat. Toss them around until golden brown, then set aside in a bowl and leave to cool.

step 2

Put the mango pulp, mint, lime juice and zest into a large bowl that fits in the fridge. Pour in the almond milk, add half of the toasted almonds and stir well.

step 3

Add the oats and mix well again, making sure the oats are covered. Cover and pop into the fridge overnight, ready for breakfast in the morning.

step 4

Next morning, take the bowl out of the fridge – the oats will have doubled in volume, as they will have soaked up all the excess moisture of the mango.

step 5

Make the topping by mixing the chopped mango and mint together. Serve in small bowls or jam jars, top with the mango, and sprinkle with the remaining toasted flaked almonds.

ingredients

100g flaked almonds

450g mango pulp

6 large mint leaves, finely chopped

1 lime, juice and zest

200ml unsweetened almond milk

150g rolled porridge oats

toppings

1 fresh mango, chopped

small handful fresh mint, finely chopped

tools

Small Frying Pan

Large Bowl

Small Bowls / Jam Jars

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
