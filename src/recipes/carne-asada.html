Carne Asada

Traditional Mexican grilled beef, marinated with citrus and spices for authentic flavor. This tender and flavorful dish is perfect for tacos, burritos, or served as a main course.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

serving

4 servings

Tags : beef dinner

instructions

step 1

Marinate the beef with lime juice, orange juice, garlic, and spices for 2 hours in the refrigerator.

step 2

Preheat grill to high heat (450-500°F). Remove beef from marinade and pat dry with paper towels.

step 3

Grill beef for 5-7 minutes per side until medium-rare (internal temperature 135°F).

step 4

Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing against the grain into thin strips.

ingredients

2 lbs flank steak

1/4 cup lime juice

1/4 cup orange juice

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp cumin

1 tsp chili powder

Salt and pepper to taste

tools

Grill

Mixing bowl

Plastic wrap

Sharp knife
